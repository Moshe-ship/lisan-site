What's inside
ما في داخل لسان
ع
Arabic-First
عربي أولاً
Explicit Arabic mode (-l ar), auto-detect with opt-in two-pass preflight for short clips, and vocabulary packs for Khaleeji / Shami / MSA biasing. One language per recording — whisper.cpp does not code-switch.
◆
Runs Locally
يعمل على جهازك
whisper.cpp with Metal GPU on Apple Silicon. Your voice never touches a server — no cloud, no API keys, no telemetry.
⬡
Press-to-Talk
اضغط وتكلّم
Hold a hotkey, speak, release. Transcript is typed directly where your cursor is — Slack, Xcode, Messages, anywhere.
⌘
Vocabulary Bias
قاموسك الخاص
Drop a plain-text file with your proper nouns, product names, Arabic dialect words. Passed as --prompt to whisper for context.
◫
Three Insertion Modes
ثلاث طرق للإدراج
CGEvent typing, Accessibility value patching, or clipboard-paste for terminals. Automatic fallback — works in every app.
⊙
Smart Cleanup
تنظيف ذكي
Rule-based cleanup engine removes filler words, fixes spacing, and applies your personal lexicon corrections (word-boundary regex).
◑
Language Modes
أوضاع اللغة
Pick EN for English, AR for Arabic, or AUTO to let Whisper detect. Switch per-session — no app restart required.
♯
Two Model Sizes
نموذجان للاختيار
ggml-base (fast, ~150MB) or ggml-small (accurate, ~500MB). Both multilingual. Ship small for Arabic quality.
⌥
Zero Subscription
بدون اشتراك
MIT licensed. No account. No usage caps. Fork it, ship it, modify the Whisper prompt. It's yours.
How it works
كيف يعمل
01 · CAPTURE
التقاط الصوت
Hold the hotkey. AVAudioEngine records into an .m4a file. Release to stop. No audio leaves the RAM of your Mac.
02 · TRANSCRIBE
النسخ محلياً
whisper-cli runs on Metal GPU. Your language mode becomes the -l flag. Your vocabulary file becomes --prompt for biasing.
03 · INSERT
الإدراج الذكي
Cleanup engine polishes the text. InsertionService picks the best transport for your frontmost app and types directly.
Privacy · خصوصية
صوتك لا يغادر جهازك. أبداً.
Your voice never leaves your Mac. No server. No telemetry. No API key. Fully offline.
